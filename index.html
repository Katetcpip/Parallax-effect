Far, far away, beyond the high mountains there is a magical forest.
That forest is full of extraordinary wonders! Tall green firs stretch to the sky, curly oaks hold fast to the ground with their roots, red-leaved maples spread their lush hair, and thin aspens flaunt each other.

Yes, yes, difficult trees live in this forest. They all know how to talk. They laugh, argue, talk or whisper to each other. A traveler who finds himself in this forest may not even notice that the trees are alive. They will stand quietly or quietly help him on his way through the forest.
